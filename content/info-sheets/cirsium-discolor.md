### aka field thistle

## _Cirsium discolor_

**Native to:** All of New England

**Life cycle:** Biennial or Short-Lived Perennial

**Light**: Full/Partial Sun

**Soil Moisture:** Medium to Medium-Dry

**Height**: 6’<br />**Spacing**: 1-2’

**Blooms**: August-October<br />
**Bloom Color:** Purple

**Natural Habitat:** Human-disturbed or -maintained habitats, meadows and fields

**Benefits**: Pollinator Favorite, Bird Favorite (eg. goldfinches), Deer/Rabbit Resistant, Host Plant (eg. Painted Lady)

> "Our native thistles tend to get grouped into the undesirable Thistle category, but they benefit many different bees, butterflies, and moths, along with hummingbirds... Goldfinches are particularly fond of the seed."
> 
> -- Prairie Moon Nursery
