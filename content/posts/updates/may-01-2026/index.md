We're going into my busiest time of the year -- market season! We'll be at the following markets in May:

## Our spring sale! 

We will be holding a **spring sale** at our home nursery. This is scheduled for **Saturday, May 9th, 2026**, from 11am-5pm, in Lunenburg, MA. Please email <a href="mailto:lise@redtrilliumgardens.com">lise@redtrilliumgardens.com</a> for the address. As this is my home, I'd like to ensure that internet randos don't show up at my door.

## Grow Native Mass Pop-up Markets

- **[Carlisle Route to Sustainability](https://www.carlislema.gov/1239/Route-to-Sustainability-Day):** Sunday, May 3, 2026, 1-4pm, Carlisle Public School gym, Carlisle, MA. We'll be joined by Checkerspot Farm, Mystic Meadows Gardens, and 1 Acre of Trees.

- **[Davis Square Farmers' Market:](https://www.facebook.com/DavisSqFarmersMarket)** Wednesday, May 20, 2026, from 12-6pm, 44 Day Street, Somerville, MA. Hopefully it will be warmer than last year's! We'll be joined by Checkerspot Farm, Butterfly Effect Farm, Tree Talk Natives, and Hilltop Natives.

## Leominster Farmers' Market

We'll be doing the **last indoor farmers' market of the season in Leominster:** Thursday, May 21st, 2026, 4-6pm, at Leominster City Hall.

## Inventory

Our [Buy Plants](/buy-plants/) page has been updated with our most recent offerings! As always, email me at [lise@redtrilliumgardens](mailto:lise@redtrilliumgardens.com) if you wish to request/reserve any plants to be picked up at one of our markets.

We hope to see you at one or more of these markets!
