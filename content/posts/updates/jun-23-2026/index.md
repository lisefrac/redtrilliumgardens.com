## Newton Tuesday Farmers' Market Opening Day

Join us for **Newton's first Tuesday farmers' market** of the season! TODAY, Tuesday 6/23/2026, from 1:30pm to 6pm we'll be at Cold Spring Park (1189 Beacon St) in Newton, MA, selling our native plants. 

Here are just a few of the great plants we'll have!

- **Wintergreen/teaberry (Gaultheria procumbens)** - a hard-to-grow groundcover that we are super excited to finally offer!
- Our **"plant vampire"** combo of slender false-foxglove (Agalinis tenuifolia) + a native grass host
- **Tall meadow-rue (Thalictrum pubescens)**, now showing off its star-like white flowers!

In addition, we will have (mostly) handmade, one-of-a-kind native bird art by **BrzBirbs**, a Lunenburg-local artist, for a very reasonable $15/each. So start thinking about what your favorite bird is! 

Hope to see you there!
